CARNNECT

A Helping Hand in the Car Community

Carnnect is a web application that brings together the stakeholders within the car community such as drivers, garages and spare parts providers.

The process of commuting to garages without sufficient knowledge/advice and also prior preparation makes the process of trying to repair your car
quite inconvinient. This is where Carnnect comes into play!

Carnnect also alows for posting of advice and experience to the people of the car community.

Carnnect also gives car owners a chance to sell their cars by posting them on the application for others to see.
